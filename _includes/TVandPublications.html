Patent:

Latent print extraction apparatus and method. I am the lead inventor and a named inventor on this patent. Granted in the US as US 12,315,289 B2, and awarded in Europe (EP 4377877). Original PCT application: WO2023007132A1. The method uses computer vision and machine learning to estimate surface curvature from reflected light patterns and correct geometric distortion in latent fingerprint imagery. Feature vectors extracted from image regions are processed using a machine learning model to identify super-pixels containing ridge detail, which are then recombined to reconstruct a curvature-corrected latent fingerprint image.
